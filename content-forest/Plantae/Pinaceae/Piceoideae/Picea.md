# Picea

**Common name:** Spruce  
**Dutch:** Spar

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Tall, conical evergreen trees with whorled branches
- **Leaves:** Short, stiff, 4-sided needles on woody pegs (sterigmata)
- **Bark:** Thin, scaly, gray to reddish-brown
- **Cones:** Pendulous, cylindrical, falling whole when mature
- **Distinguishing:** Needles on woody pegs; cones hang down and fall intact
