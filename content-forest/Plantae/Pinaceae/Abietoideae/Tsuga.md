# Tsuga

**Common name:** Hemlock  
**Dutch:** Hemlockspar

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Graceful evergreen trees with drooping branch tips and leader
- **Leaves:** Short, flat needles with two white stripes beneath, on small stalks
- **Bark:** Deeply furrowed, reddish-brown to gray, rich in tannin
- **Cones:** Small, pendulous, ovoid, persistent
- **Distinguishing:** Delicate drooping form; short-stalked needles; small cones
