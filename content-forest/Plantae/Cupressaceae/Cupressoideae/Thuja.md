# Thuja

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/index|Cupressoideae]]

**Common name:** Arborvitae, Thuja  
**Dutch:** Levensboom

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen trees or shrubs with flattened, fan-like foliage sprays
- **Leaves:** Scale-like in opposite pairs, aromatic when crushed
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, fibrous, often spirally furrowed
- **Cones:** Small, elongated, with 8-12 thin, overlapping scales
- **Distinguishing:** Strongly flattened sprays; distinctive sweet/resinous scent
