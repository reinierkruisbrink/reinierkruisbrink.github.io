# Cupressus

**Common name:** True cypress  
**Dutch:** Cipres

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen trees or large shrubs with dense, often columnar crowns
- **Leaves:** Scale-like, tightly pressed to stem in opposite pairs
- **Bark:** Thin, fibrous, often peeling in strips
- **Cones:** Globose, woody, with shield-shaped scales
- **Distinguishing:** Cones mature in two years; seeds with narrow wings
