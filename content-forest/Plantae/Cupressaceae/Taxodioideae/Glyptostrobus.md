# Glyptostrobus

**Common name:** Chinese swamp cypress  
**Dutch:** Chinese moerasCipres

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Small to medium deciduous conifer, critically endangered
- **Leaves:** Dimorphic - scale-like on permanent shoots, linear on deciduous branchlets
- **Bark:** Gray-brown, fissured
- **Cones:** Pear-shaped, with distinctive winged scales
- **Distinguishing:** Only living species; produces knee-like projections in water
