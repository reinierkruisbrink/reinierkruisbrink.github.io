# Metasequoia

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Cupressaceae/Sequoioideae/index|Sequoioideae]]

**Common name:** Dawn redwood  
**Dutch:** Watercipres

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Tall, deciduous conifer with conical crown
- **Leaves:** Flat, soft needles arranged oppositely, bright green turning bronze in autumn
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, fibrous, deeply furrowed
- **Cones:** Small, pendulous, on long stalks
- **Distinguishing:** Only deciduous redwood; opposite leaf arrangement
