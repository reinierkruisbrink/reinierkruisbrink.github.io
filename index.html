Who am I?

Curious // Resourceful // Analytic // Goal Oriented

I'm passionate about exploring the patterns and relationships that underlie our world, driving my curiosity in both technology and science. Beyond the realms of technology, I find inspiration in the fascinating domains of biology, physics, and chemistry, especially in bioinformatics and at the intersection of AI and life & health sciences. Additionally, my interest in linguistics adds to my belief that, while language is subjective, commonalities in language usage expose a richer and more open way of understanding and sharing knowledge. Similarly, advances in neuroscience (and behaviours such as perception and action) enable better communication and understanding by realising the similarities and differencesand their respective consequences accross people. I firmly believe that the synergy and complementarity across different fields are rich sources of knowledge, pushing the boundaries of understanding.
