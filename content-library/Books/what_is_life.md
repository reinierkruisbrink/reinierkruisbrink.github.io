# What is Life? Five Great Ideas in Biology

## Overview

In "What is Life?", Nobel laureate Paul Nurse tackles the fundamental question of what defines life. The book isn't a rigid scientific treatise, but rather a journey of discovery through five key biological concepts:

## Key Concepts

(1) The Cell: The basic unit of life, cells are complex and organized entities separate from their environment.
                  
 (2) The Gene: These blueprints carry hereditary information and are crucial for reproduction and variation.
                  
 (3) Evolution by Natural Selection: This theory explains how life adapts and changes over time.
                  
 (4) Life as Chemistry: Underlying all biological processes are intricate chemical reactions.
                  
 (5) Life as Information: Living things store and use information encoded in molecules like DNA.
