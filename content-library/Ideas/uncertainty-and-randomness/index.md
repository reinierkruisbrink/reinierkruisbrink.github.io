- Probability is a tool for reasoning under uncertainty, not a description of the world — frequentist vs Bayesian framing.
- How institutions (insurance, diversification, redundancy) are essentially systems for managing uncertainty rather than eliminating it.
- Connection to predictive processing: the brain as a machine that minimises surprise — uncertainty as the thing to reduce.
