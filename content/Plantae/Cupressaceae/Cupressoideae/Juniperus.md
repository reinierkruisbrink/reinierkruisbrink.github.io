# Juniperus

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/index|Cupressoideae]]

**Common name:** Juniper  
**Dutch:** Jeneverbes

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen trees or shrubs, highly variable in form
- **Leaves:** Either needle-like (juvenile) or scale-like (mature), often both on same plant
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, thin, shredding
- **Cones:** Berry-like, fleshy, blue-black when ripe (used to flavor gin)
- **Distinguishing:** Fleshy "berries" instead of woody cones; aromatic foliage
