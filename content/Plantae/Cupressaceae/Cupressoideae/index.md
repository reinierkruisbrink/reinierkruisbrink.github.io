# Cupressoideae

*Parent: [[Cupressaceae/index|Cupressaceae]]*

The true cypress subfamily is the largest and most diverse group within Cupressaceae. Members are characterized by scale-like leaves and globose to oblong woody cones. Many species are cultivated as ornamentals or for timber.

## Genera

- [[Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/Cupressus|Cupressus]] - True cypresses
- [[Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/Chamaecyparis|Chamaecyparis]] - False cypresses
- [[Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/Juniperus|Juniperus]] - Junipers
- [[Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/Thuja|Thuja]] - Arborvitae
- [[Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae/Platycladus|Platycladus]] - Oriental arborvitae
