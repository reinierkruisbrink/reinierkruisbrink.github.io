# Chamaecyparis

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Cupressaceae/Cupressoideae]]

**Common name:** False cypress  
**Dutch:** Schijncipres

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen trees with flattened, spray-like foliage branches
- **Leaves:** Scale-like in opposite pairs, forming flat sprays
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, fibrous, often furrowed
- **Cones:** Small, globose, with 6-12 scales
- **Distinguishing:** Smaller cones than true cypress; flattened branch sprays
