# Taxodium

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Cupressaceae/Taxodioideae]]

**Common name:** Bald cypress  
**Dutch:** Moerascipres

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Large deciduous conifer adapted to swamps and flooded areas
- **Leaves:** Flat, feathery, bright green turning russet in autumn
- **Bark:** Gray-brown, fibrous, often with a buttressed base
- **Cones:** Globose, breaking apart at maturity
- **Distinguishing:** Produces woody "knees" (pneumatophores) in wet conditions
