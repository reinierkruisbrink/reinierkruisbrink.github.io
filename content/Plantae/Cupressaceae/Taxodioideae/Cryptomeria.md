# Cryptomeria

*Parent: [[Taxodioideae]]*

**Common name:** Japanese cedar, Sugi  
**Dutch:** Japanse ceder

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Tall evergreen with pyramidal crown, important timber tree in Japan
- **Leaves:** Awl-shaped, curved, spirally arranged, bright green
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, fibrous, peeling in strips
- **Cones:** Globose, spiny, with hooked scale tips
- **Distinguishing:** Leaves curve inward toward stem; national tree of Japan
