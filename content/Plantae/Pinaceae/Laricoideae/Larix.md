# Larix

*Parent: [[Laricoideae]]*

**Common name:** Larch  
**Dutch:** Lariks

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Tall, deciduous conifer with conical crown turning golden in autumn
- **Leaves:** Soft, bright green needles in dense clusters on short shoots
- **Bark:** Deeply furrowed, reddish-brown to gray, thick and fire-resistant
- **Cones:** Small, upright, persistent, with reflexed bracts visible
- **Distinguishing:** Deciduous (rare for conifers); clustered soft needles
