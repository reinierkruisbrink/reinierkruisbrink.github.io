# Platycladus

*Parent: [[Cupressoideae]]*

**Common name:** Oriental arborvitae, Chinese thuja  
**Dutch:** Chinese levensboom

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Small evergreen tree or large shrub with vertical foliage sprays
- **Leaves:** Scale-like, in opposite pairs, forming flattened vertical fans
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, thin, shredding
- **Cones:** Distinctive fleshy, blue-green before maturity, with hooked scale tips
- **Distinguishing:** Foliage sprays held vertically (not horizontally like Thuja)
