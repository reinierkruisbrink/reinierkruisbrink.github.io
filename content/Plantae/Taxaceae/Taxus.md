# Taxus

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Taxaceae]]

**Common name:** Yew  
**Dutch:** Taxus

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen trees or shrubs, often multi-stemmed, extremely long-lived
- **Leaves:** Flat, dark green needles in two ranks, with pale undersides
- **Bark:** Reddish-brown, thin, scaly, flaking
- **Seeds:** Surrounded by fleshy red aril (berry-like), highly poisonous except aril
- **Distinguishing:** No true cones; all parts toxic except red aril flesh
