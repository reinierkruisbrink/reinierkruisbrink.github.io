# Cephalotaxus

**Parent:** [[Plantae/Taxaceae]]

**Common name:** Plum yew  
**Dutch:** Pruimtaxus

## Identifying Features

- **Habit:** Evergreen shrubs or small trees with spreading branches
- **Leaves:** Long, flat, dark green needles in two ranks, larger than Taxus
- **Bark:** Gray-brown, peeling in strips
- **Seeds:** Large, plum-like, olive-green to purple when ripe
- **Distinguishing:** Larger needles and seeds than Taxus; seeds resemble small plums
